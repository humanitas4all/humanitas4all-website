Mission Statement

Founded in 2018, Humanitas4All aims to provide STEM enrichment opportunities for low-income and Title I communities around the DC, Maryland, and Virginia area. Through our tutoring services in local community centers, Humanitas4All connects volunteers willing to make a difference with students without proper access to educational materials and basic necessities.

We connect with students participating in local community center programs and allow high school volunteers the opporunity to contribute to these student's education.

Learn more about our individual projects and ways to contribute to Humanitas4All by visiting our Projects and Get Involved pages.
